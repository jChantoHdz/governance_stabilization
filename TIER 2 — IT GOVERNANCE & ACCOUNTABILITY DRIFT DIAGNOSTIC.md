# TIER 2 — IT GOVERNANCE & ACCOUNTABILITY DRIFT DIAGNOSTIC

**Ideal for:**  
Mid-market organizations experiencing accountability ambiguity, KPI proliferation without enforcement, rising cloud costs without clarity, or board scrutiny regarding IT governance maturity.

**Engagement Model:**  
Four-week structured executive assessment  
Executive interviews, KPI mapping, governance review, and board-ready reporting

**Investment:**  
18,000 – 30,000 USD per engagement

## Duties and Responsibilities:

- Conduct KPI-to-ownership mapping across IT functions  
- Identify decision-right ambiguity and accountability gaps  
- Assess governance maturity across DevOps, AI, and cloud programs  
- Review executive reporting clarity and escalation pathways  
- Evaluate cloud cost ownership transparency  
- Analyze accountability enforcement mechanisms  
- Identify governance drift indicators and structural fragility  

## Engagement Outcomes:

- Governance Drift Indicator Scorecard  
- Accountability Clarity Matrix  
- Executive visibility gap assessment  
- Cloud cost ownership risk summary  
- 9–12 month Governance Reset Roadmap  
- Board-ready executive summary
