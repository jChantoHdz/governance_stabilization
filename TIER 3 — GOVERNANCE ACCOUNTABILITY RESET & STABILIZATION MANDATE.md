# TIER 3 — GOVERNANCE ACCOUNTABILITY RESET & STABILIZATION MANDATE

**Ideal for:**  
Mid-market organizations where governance maturity has fallen behind operational scale, KPIs lack enforceable ownership, and executive trust in IT discipline is eroding.

**Engagement Model:**  
Four to eight month executive-led stabilization mandate  
Structured milestone framework with board-level reporting

**Investment:**  
110,000 – 160,000 USD per engagement

## Duties and Responsibilities:

- Redesign IT accountability structures and ownership models  
- Realign decision-right authority across technology functions  
- Embed enforceable KPI ownership discipline  
- Implement cloud cost governance and accountability controls  
- Formalize AI oversight frameworks with defined ownership  
- Redesign executive reporting and governance dashboards  
- Establish escalation pathways and accountability enforcement mechanisms  
- Strengthen board-facing transparency structures  

## Engagement Outcomes:

- Restored accountability clarity across IT programs  
- Reduced operational ambiguity and ownership confusion  
- Improved cloud cost visibility and discipline  
- Stabilized governance structures during scale  
- Increased delivery predictability  
- Rebuilt executive and board confidence
