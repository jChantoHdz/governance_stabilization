# TIER 5 — INTERIM EXECUTIVE GOVERNANCE STABILIZATION

**Ideal for:**  
Organizations experiencing leadership gaps, governance breakdown, accountability erosion, or executive pressure regarding IT discipline.

**Engagement Model:**  
Embedded executive mandate  
Minimum three-month commitment  
Executive and board reporting participation

**Investment:**  
25,000 – 40,000 USD per month

## Duties and Responsibilities:

- Conduct immediate KPI-to-ownership audit  
- Restore decision-right clarity within first 30 days  
- Stabilize executive visibility into cost and performance metrics  
- Reset cloud cost accountability structures  
- Formalize AI oversight and governance enforcement  
- Implement 30-60-90 day governance stabilization plan  
- Reinforce board-level reporting discipline  
- Develop internal successor capability before transition  

## Engagement Outcomes:

- Immediate governance stabilization  
- Restored accountability clarity  
- Improved executive confidence in IT discipline  
- Reduced cost exposure and ownership ambiguity  
- Structured leadership transition continuity
