# Executive IT Governance & Accountability Stabilization Portfolio

## Positioning

Executive IT Governance & Accountability Stabilizer for mid-market organizations experiencing governance drift within their technology functions.

This portfolio is designed for organizations where:

- KPIs exist but ownership is unclear
- Cloud costs increase without executive visibility
- AI initiatives accelerate without structured oversight
- Boards question IT accountability
- Leadership transitions weaken governance discipline

The focus is not tooling modernization.

The focus is restoring decision clarity, accountability discipline, cost transparency, and executive confidence.

---

## Engagement Structure

- Tier 1 — Executive IT Governance & Accountability Oversight
- Tier 2 — IT Governance & Accountability Drift Diagnostic
- Tier 3 — Governance Accountability Reset & Stabilization Mandate
- Tier 4 — Governance Leadership & Accountability Discipline Program
- Tier 5 — Interim Executive Governance Stabilization

Each tier is designed to address governance maturity at increasing levels of operational intensity.

Primary market focus: North America mid-market and select LATAM engagements.
