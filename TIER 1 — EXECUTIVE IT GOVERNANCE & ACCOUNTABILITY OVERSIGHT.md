# TIER 1 — EXECUTIVE IT GOVERNANCE & ACCOUNTABILITY OVERSIGHT

**Ideal for:**  
CIOs, CTOs, CEOs, and Boards of mid-market organizations where KPI ownership clarity is weakening, cost visibility is limited, or executive confidence in IT governance is declining.

**Engagement Model:**  
Monthly executive retainer  
8–12 hours per month  
Bi-weekly executive governance sessions plus on-demand advisory support

**Investment:**  
7,000 – 10,000 USD per month

## Duties and Responsibilities:

- Review and reinforce KPI-to-owner alignment across IT programs  
- Assess and clarify decision-right structures within technology leadership  
- Strengthen executive reporting frameworks for cost and accountability visibility  
- Monitor cloud cost exposure and ownership discipline  
- Provide governance drift early-warning assessments  
- Advise on accountability risks related to AI and DevOps scaling  
- Support board-level IT accountability discussions  
- Guide leadership teams in enforcing measurable ownership discipline  

## Engagement Outcomes:

- Clear ownership alignment across critical IT programs  
- Improved executive visibility into cloud and AI cost exposure  
- Reduced governance drift risk  
- Strengthened accountability discipline  
- Increased board confidence in IT control structures
